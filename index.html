04 — Methodology

WORKING
METHOD

Phase I

Read the Site

Map slope, access, weather, views, and existing constraints before deciding what the building should do.

Phase II

Draw Fast

Test massing, routes, and room proportions before the project becomes too polished to question.

Phase III

Resolve Structure

Keep structure legible. Walls, spans, and openings should read as one decision, not separate layers.

Phase IV

Choose Materials

Use materials that fit the climate, budget, maintenance load, and the tone of the place.

Phase V

Detail the Edges

Junctions, thresholds, drainage, and shade lines decide whether the project holds together.
